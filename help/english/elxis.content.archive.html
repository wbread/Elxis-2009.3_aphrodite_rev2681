Archive Manager

Shows all archived content items. You can filter the displayed items by Section, Category, Author and Language. You can also use the 'Filter' input box.

You can inspect archived items or unarchive them. Unarchive means, that you move an item from archive back to active state. Unarchived items are by default unpublished.

 

Toolbar Icons

Unarchive: Removes selected items from archive and returns them to their original location.

Trash: Moves selected items to Trash Bin.

 

Columns

Title: Content item's title.

Order: Shows current order of archived content items. You can change it, by editing the order numbers and then clicking the "Save Order" icon next to column header.

Category: Category archived item belongs to. Click to edit the Category.

Author: Archived item's author. Click to edit author.

Date: The date content item was created.

Language: Active language(s) for this item.
