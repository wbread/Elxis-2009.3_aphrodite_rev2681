Categories Manager: Copy/Move

This page lets you move or copy a Category and its Content Items to another Section.

 

Toolbar Icons

Save: Copy/Moves the Category to the selected Section and returns to Categories Manager.

Cancel: Cancels the action.

Help: This Help Screen.

 

Copy/Move Category

Copy/Move to Section: This list shows all the available Sections. Select the Section you want to copy/move Category to.

Categories being copied/moved: Shows the Categories that will be copied/moved.

Content Items being copied/moved: Shows the Content Items that will be copied/moved.
