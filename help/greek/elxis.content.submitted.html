Submitted content items are articles that have been submitted from your site's Front-End. These items are stored in a temporary area till you inpect and publish them. You should consider title, seotitle, section, category, language and meta data as suggested data. You are the one that take the decision if and where (section/category) these items will be published.

 

Publish submitted content item

To publish a User Submitted Content Item just edit it. By clicking on the save or apply icon buttons User Submitted Content Items are moved from the temporary storage area to the selected section/category and afterwards are considered as normal content.

 

Delete / Reject submitted content item

To delete (reject) a User Submitted Content Item just select it and press delete. Deleted user submitted content items are not moved to trash!

 

Comments

Frontend users have the option to attach comments to a content item. These comments are addressed to you, the inspector. Please consider them when reviewing a submitted item as they may contain useful information about the user's intensions when writing that article.

 

Toolbar Icons

Edit: Click to edit selected Submitted Item.

Delete: Click to delete selected Submitted Item. Deleted Submitted Items are not moved to Trash Bin.

Help: This Help Screen.

 

Columns

Title: Suggested title by author. You can change it when editing.

SEO title: Suggested SEO title by Elxis. You can change it when editing.

Section: Suggested section this item should be published. You can change it when editing.

Category: Suggested category this item should be published. You can change it when editing.

Author: The author of the suggested item.

Language: Always set to 'All'. You can change it when editing.
