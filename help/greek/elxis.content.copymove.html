Διαχειριστής Αντικειμένων Περιεχομένου:
Μετακίνηση/Αντιγραφή Αντικειμένων

Using this screen, you can Move or Copy selected content items from one category to another. When you copy, then new content items are created in the selected category. When you move, then content items are move to the selected category.

 

Toolbar

Save: Performs the Copy or Move operation.

Cancel: Cancel and return to previous screen without performing the operation.

Help: This Help Screen.

 

Move to Section/Category

Lists all available categories in the form of '[section] / [category]'. Click to select the category, items will be copied/moved to.

 

Items being Moved

Shows the content items that are about to be moved/copied.
