Διαχειριστής Κάδου Ανακύκλωσης

This screen shows the list of Content Items and Menu Items that are in the "Trash bin". Trash bin is the equivalent of the "Recycle bin" in Ms Windows. You can restore trashed items or delete them permanently.

Trash Manager has two tabs.
Content Items tab, lists all Content Items that have been trashed.
Menu Items tab, lists all Menu Items that have been trashed.

 

Toolbar

Restore: Select an item and click the Restore icon to undelete it. The item will be returned to its original position and by default, it will be unpublished.

Delete: Select an item and click the delete icon, to permanently delete it.

Help: This Help Screen

 

Tables

Tab Content Items

Title: This is the title of the trashed Content Item.

Section: This is the section the trashed Content Item belongs.

Category: This is the category the trashed Content Item belongs.

ID: This is the ID of the trashed Content Item.

Tab Menu Items

Title: Trashed Menu Item's name.

Menu: The menu this Menu Item belongs.

Type: The type of Menu Item.

ID: Menu Item's ID.
