Help: This Help Screen.

 

General

Lock Inbox: Allows or prevents Elxis and Administrators to send you messages. It is a good idea not to lock the inbox (set it to "No").

Mail me on new Message: Allows or prevents Elxis to send you a notification email when you have a new message. The recommended setting is "Yes".
